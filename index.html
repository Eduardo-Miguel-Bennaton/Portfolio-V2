About me

Biography

Born on July 16th, 2004, I was raised, and educated in Tegucigalpa, Honduras. I grew up in a family of 3 (including me), and spent the majority of my life going to school and participating in multi-cultural events that flourished my education.

When I turned 17, I began to pursue a better formation abroad. I flew to Cloppenburg, Germany on September 31st, where I would spend one year studying German at Liebfrauenschule as an exchange student.
